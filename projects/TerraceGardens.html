Terrace Gardens

Ã—

For this project, I designed a set of terrace gardens using SketchUp 3D software, combining visual planning with practical layout considerations. The design focused on maximizing green space in elevated or urban settings, integrating planter boxes, walkways, and seating areas into a cohesive and functional environment.

The construction process involved careful use of materials suited for outdoor conditions, with attention to drainage, accessibility, and maintenance. The project emphasized sustainability, using reclaimed wood and durable components where possible to reduce environmental impact while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

This project demonstrates how digital tools like SketchUp can aid in conceptualizing and communicating green space designs before physical work begins. The resulting gardens not only offer a peaceful retreat but also promote urban biodiversity and efficient use of vertical space.
